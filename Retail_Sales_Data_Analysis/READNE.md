# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a real-world retail dataset to understand business performance, customer behavior, and key revenue drivers. The goal is to extract meaningful insights that can support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall revenue performance  
- Identify top-performing products and customers  
- Understand country-wise market contribution  
- Detect seasonal trends in sales  
- Study customer purchasing behavior  
- Identify data quality issues and outliers  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📂 Dataset

- Online Retail Dataset  
- Source: UCI Machine Learning Repository  

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue Analysis
- Total revenue calculation  
- Top revenue-generating products  
- Revenue distribution analysis  

### 2. Product Performance
- High-demand products (quantity)  
- High-price vs low-sales products  
- Low-revenue high-frequency products  

### 3. Country / Market Analysis
- Top countries by revenue  
- Low-performing markets  
- Revenue contribution by country  

### 4. Time-Based Analysis
- Monthly revenue trends  
- Peak sales months  
- Seasonal patterns  

### 5. Customer Behavior Analysis
- Top customers by revenue  
- Revenue concentration (Pareto principle)  
- Average revenue per customer  

### 6. Transaction Analysis
- Average order value (AOV)  
- Bulk transactions  
- Order size distribution  

### 7. Data Quality & Outliers
- Negative values detection  
- Outlier analysis  
- Impact of outliers on results  

### 8. Relationship Analysis
- Price vs Revenue  
- Quantity vs Revenue  
- Country-wise pricing behavior  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Revenue is highly concentrated among a small number of products and customers  
- A few countries contribute the majority of sales  
- Sales follow seasonal trends with peak months  
- Most transactions are small, but large orders significantly impact revenue  
- Customer behavior follows the 80/20 rule  
- Data quality issues like outliers and negative values affect analysis  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing products and customers  
- Diversify markets to reduce dependency on specific countries  
- Use seasonal trends for better inventory and marketing planning  
- Increase average order value through bundling and upselling  
- Improve data cleaning processes for accurate analysis
